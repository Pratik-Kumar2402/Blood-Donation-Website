    who can give blood?

    Most people can give blood if they are in good health.there are some basic requirement one need to fulfil in order to become a blood donor . below are some basic eligibility guideline

    Age

    you are age between 18 and 65.

      in some countries,donors of whole blood donation should weigh at least 45 kg to donate 350ml+-10%.

    Health

    you must be in good health at time you donate.
    you cannot donateif you have a cold sore,flu,sore throat,stomach bug any other infection.


    if you have recently had a tattoo or body piercing you cannot donate for 6 month from the date of the procedure .if the body
    piercimng was performed by a register health propffesional and any inflammation has settled completly,you can donate bloodafter 12 hours

    you must donate blood if you do not meet the minimum haemoglobin level for blood donation :

    Behaviour

    you must not give blood

    • if you engaged in "at risk" sexual activity in the pasty 12 month .
    • have ever had a positive test for HIV AIDS virus
    • Have ever injected recreational drugs.

    Pregnancy and breastfeeding
    • it is not advisable to dopnate blood while breast-feeding . Following childbirth,the deferral period is at least 9 month (as for preganancy) and until 3 months after your baby is significantly weaned (getting most of his/her nutrition from solid or bottle feeding

    The common reason donors should check if they can give blood are:
